  • About Me

    Hello, my name is Stacy Rahall. I was born and raised in Fayetteville, West Virginia. I knew I was destined to cut hair when I decided to cut my own at age three :)

    I've been a licensed cosmetologist since 2007. I ran my own hair salon in West Virginia for nine years before moving to Colorado. That's where my journey began with barbering, working beside some of the best barbers in Boulder. I use a wide variety of techniques I've learned from both working in hair salons, as well as a barber shops. I specialize in haircutting for the whole family!

    I accept cash, Venmo, and credit card for payment. Your card on file is only used for no shows, not for payment. Please have a payment method ready during your service.

    Outside of work, you can find me hiking with my two dogs, Kaya and Odie.

    Thank you to all my loyal customers over the years for helping me make this happen. Hope to see you soon!

    - Stacy
