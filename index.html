  • About Me

    Hello, my name is Stacy Licata. I was born and raised in Fayetteville, West Virginia. My interest in the beauty industry started when I decided to cut my own hair when I was three years old :)

    I've been a licensed Cosmetologist since 2007. I ran my own hair salon in West Virginia for nine years before moving to Colorado. That's where my journey began with barbering, working beside some of the best barbers in Boulder.

    I'm excited offer a wide variety of hair services at my new studio! I offer cuts for all ages, from kiddos to grandparents, coloring and styling for all types of hair, and top of the line barbering techniques to keep you looking your best! If you're interested, you can check out some of my work by visiting my Instagram!

    Outside of work, you can find me hanging out with my husband Noah (who also made this awesome website!) and our two dogs, Kaya and Odie. Our dog scooter ride is the highlight of our days! I'll be posting videos of it here soon, so stay tuned! If you live in Broomfield, you might have seen us riding around! :)

    Thank you to all my loyal customers over the years for helping me make this happen. Hope to see you soon!

    - Stacy
